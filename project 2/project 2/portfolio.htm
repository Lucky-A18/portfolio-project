üçù Favorite Meal 1: Spaghetti Carbonara

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 100g pancetta
  • 2 eggs
  • 50g Parmesan cheese
  • Black pepper

Instructions

  1. Cook the spaghetti according to package instructions.
  2. Fry the pancetta until crispy.
  3. Whisk the eggs with grated Parmesan and black pepper.
  4. Mix the pasta with the pancetta, then add the egg mixture and stir to create a creamy sauce.

üçó Favorite Meal 2: Chicken Stir Fry

Ingredients

  • 2 chicken breasts
  • 1 bell pepper
  • 1 onion
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil

Instructions

  1. Slice the chicken into strips and cook in a pan with sesame oil.
  2. Add sliced bell peppers and onions to the pan.
  3. Pour in the soy sauce and stir until everything is cooked through.
